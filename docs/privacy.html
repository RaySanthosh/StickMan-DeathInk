Privacy Policy — Death Note

Last updated: 3 July 2026

This policy explains what the mobile game Death Note ("the game", "we") collects, why, and how you can remove it. By playing the game you agree to this policy.

Playing without an account

You can download and play Chapter 1 as an anonymous guest. In this mode we do not collect your name or email. Your progress is stored only on your device.

Information we collect when you sign in

To play beyond Chapter 1 and appear on the global leaderboard, you sign in with Google. When you do, we collect and store:

  • Your Google account email address — used to identify your account.
  • A display name and country that you choose — shown publicly on the leaderboard.
  • Gameplay results — your completion time and number of deaths per chapter.
  • A notification (FCM) token — an anonymous device identifier used to send you game notifications (for example, weekly updates). You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time.

We do not collect your phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, or any other personal data. Country is self-selected by you, not detected.

How your information is used

  • To show the public leaderboard (display name, country, time, deaths).
  • To save and sync your progress across sessions.
  • To send you optional game notifications.
  • To understand aggregate, anonymous usage of the game (via Google Analytics for Firebase).

We do not sell your data, and we do not share it with third parties for advertising. The game contains no ads.

Where your data is stored

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Cloud Messaging and Analytics). Data is transmitted over encrypted connections. Firebase's handling of data is governed by Google's privacy terms.

Deleting your data

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from inside the game: open Profile → Delete account. This immediately removes your profile, leaderboard scores and notification token from our database.

You may also request deletion by email — see our Account Deletion page.

Children

The game is intended for players aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
