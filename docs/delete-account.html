Delete Your Death Note Account

You can permanently delete your account and all of your data at any time.

In the app (fastest)

  1. Open Death Note.
  2. Go to Profile (the "Sign in" / "Profile ✓" button on the main screen).
  3. Tap Delete account and confirm.

This immediately and permanently removes your profile, your leaderboard scores, and your notification token from our database.

By email

If you can no longer access the app, email iyappansanthosh2004@gmail.com from the Google account you signed in with, with the subject "Delete my account". We will delete your data within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account (Google sign-in link).
  • Your profile: display name, email and country.
  • All of your leaderboard scores (completion times and death counts).
  • Your notification (FCM) token.

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
