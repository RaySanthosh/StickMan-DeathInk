# Privacy Policy for Dead Ink

**Last updated:** July 4, 2026

Santhosh Iyappan ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Dead Ink mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you sign in with Google, we receive your display name, email address, and profile photo URL from Google. This information is used solely to identify your account and display your name on leaderboards.

### Game Data
We store your game progress (completed levels, scores, and chapter unlocks) in Firebase Cloud Firestore, linked to your account. This data is used to sync your progress across devices and populate leaderboards.

### Crash and Performance Data
We may collect anonymous crash reports and performance metrics through Firebase to improve the App's stability.

## How We Use Your Information

- To authenticate your identity via Google Sign-In
- To save and sync your game progress
- To display leaderboard rankings
- To improve app stability and performance

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is only processed through Google Firebase services, which operate under [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account exists. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game content (stylized stickman platformer with cartoon-style hazards) contains no realistic violence, gore, or mature themes.

## Data Security

We use Firebase's built-in security rules and HTTPS encryption to protect your data in transit and at rest.

## Your Rights

You may:
- Request a copy of your stored data
- Request deletion of your account and associated data
- Opt out by not signing in (the App works offline without an account)
